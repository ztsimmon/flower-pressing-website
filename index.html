About Us

Xavonne has been passionate about preserving the beauty of flowers through the art of pressing for over a decade. Her work transforms fresh flowers into beautiful, lasting keepsakes. Whether for weddings, funerals, or other special occasions, each piece is crafted with love and meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that your memories are preserved beautifully. She combines traditional techniques with creative designs to offer unique and personalized artwork that clients cherish.
